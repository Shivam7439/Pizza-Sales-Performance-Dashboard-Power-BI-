Pizza-Sales-Performance-Dashboard-Power-BI-

## 🧾 Introduction

This project presents a comprehensive Pizza Sales Performance Dashboard built using Power BI.
The dataset contains 1 year of sales data (~3500 rows) including order details, customer behavior, and sales metrics.

## 📌 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Sales:** 360,847
* **Total Orders:** 3,500
* **Total Quantity Sold:** 10,571
* **Average Order Value:** 1,031

These KPIs provide a quick overview of overall business performance.

## 📈 Sales Trend Analysis

The monthly sales trend shows fluctuations throughout the year.

* Peak sales were observed in **May and October**, indicating high demand during these periods.
* Lower sales were recorded in months like **February and September**, suggesting possible seasonal dips.

This indicates that marketing efforts can be increased during low-performing months.

## ⏰ Time-Based Analysis

* The highest number of orders were placed during the **evening hours**, making it the peak business time.
* Morning orders are relatively lower, indicating less customer activity.

This insight suggests focusing promotions and offers during non-peak hours to balance demand.

## 📅 Day-wise Order Analysis

* Orders are fairly consistent across all days of the week.
* Slightly higher sales are observed during **weekends**, indicating increased customer activity.

This highlights the importance of weekend promotions and staffing.

## 🍕 Product Performance

* Top-performing products include **Paneer Pizza, Veggie Pizza, and Farmhouse Pizza**.
* Lower-performing items include **Chocolate Pizza and Hawaiian Pizza**.

This suggests focusing more on popular items while re-evaluating underperforming products.

## 🥗 Category-wise Analysis

* **Veg category** contributes the highest share of sales.
* **Non-Veg category** follows closely.
* **Dessert category** has the lowest contribution.

This indicates customer preference is more towards regular meal options rather than desserts.

## 💡 Key Insights

* Peak sales occur during evening hours and weekends.
* Certain products consistently outperform others.
* Seasonal trends impact monthly sales performance.
* Customer preference leans towards Veg options.
